# PaidPolitely Email Routing Notes

The website needs a visible public contact email and a catch-all for future aliases.

## Required inbox

```txt
ads@paidpolitely.com
```

Use this as the public CTA for:

- buying display ads
- requesting rates
- sponsor packages
- subreddit onboarding conversations
- mod/community introductions

## Recommended catch-all

```txt
*@paidpolitely.com
```

The catch-all is useful for later aliases such as:

```txt
mods@paidpolitely.com
partners@paidpolitely.com
support@paidpolitely.com
hello@paidpolitely.com
billing@paidpolitely.com
```

## DNS/provider note

Next.js does not provide email hosting. Configure this with your domain registrar, Cloudflare Email Routing, Google Workspace, Fastmail, Proton, Zoho, or another mail provider.
